

This is the locked solo author edition of the AuthorVAULT master blueprint. It covers every record type a solo self-published author needs ΓÇö from company formation through individual platform upload logs ΓÇö including expanded records for KDP Select enrollment, box sets and bundles, ARC and launch management, promotional campaigns and newsletter swaps, awards and recognition, and subscription/serialization platforms.

Publisher Dashboard features (multi-author management, author accounts, acquisition contracts, royalty accounting) are designated Phase II and are not included in this document.






The Publishing Company is the root of the entire AuthorVAULT tree. Every imprint, pen name, series, and book flows from it.







An imprint is the publishing brand that appears on book covers, copyright pages, and retailer listings.
















A box set is a first-class publishing product that contains references to existing book records. It has its own cover, ISBN, platform listings, pricing, and metadata. It lives at the Series level because it is a product of the series, not an edition of a single book.



Box set Language / Edition Branch, Format, and Platform Variant records follow the identical structure as individual books.










One record per contributor ΓÇö name, role, company/agency, email, phone, website, credit line, contract file, contract date, payment terms, fee amount, payment method, due date, delivery date, QA/approval status, and internal notes.













One record per promotional run. Enables the author to build a performance database that informs future spending and integrates directly with ScribeCount revenue data.






One record per submission event. Tracks both operational workflow (submission, result) and marketing assets (seal, badge, quotes) generated by each award.


Lives at the Book level, parallel to Language/Edition branches. Covers Kindle Vella, Ream, Patreon, Radish, Wattpad, Royal Road, and similar platforms where the book's content is published in episodes or chapters under a subscription or unlock model.




















Lives inside the Ebook Format node. KDP Select enrollment is format-specific ΓÇö an English ebook may be KU-enrolled while a Spanish ebook is distributed wide. One enrollment record per 90-day period.














The Language Index is a filtered navigation view at the Pen Name level. It does not store data ΓÇö it reads from existing Language Branch records and presents them grouped by language. One source of truth, two navigation paths.
